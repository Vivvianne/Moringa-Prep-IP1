Bio

Vivvianne Kimani was born in Nairobi. Since her parents were civil servants, her family move from Nairobi to Busia to Isebania, and to Kisumu.

All this while she was doing her primary and secondary schooling. She finished this and joined University of Nairobi to do Geography and economics.Later on she did a Master of Arts in Environmental management in the same institution.

Vivvianne is looking forward to building apps and engaging in projects that will improve the livelihoods of people and make the world a better place.
